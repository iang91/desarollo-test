How To Use:
Masks:
  1. Apply masks in the suggested sequence or in any order, one per day for seven consecutive days or individually as needed.
  2. Remove makeup, cleanse, and dry your face.
  3. Open and remove the mask, unfold it, and align it with your eyes and mouth.
  4. Once the sheet mask is settled comfortably on your face, roll this smooth, polished stone tool over the material in an outward motion to distribute the serum, increase blood flow, de-puff and de-bloat skin, and
  5. even help you relax through the art of facial massage.
  6. Remove the mask after 15-20 minutes.
  7. Allow any remaining concentration to absorb into the skin for 2-3 minutes. Smooth in any remaining content.
DEEP HYDRATION Aloe Gel Mist:
  1. Apply to the face or body anytime skin needs a boost.
  2. Close the eyes and spray directly onto the face or body as needed.
